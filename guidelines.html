Guidelines Before Submitting your Fanart

(because common sense apparently doesn't exist)


Please follow the guidelines below before submitting your art, there aren't a lot, so no worries

1) Always use the template and it's means

This is the template, which is automatically generated in your e-mail inbox, and what they mean:

Title: (however you want to title your art)
Your username: (it's obvious)
Any socials you have (including your own site: (this is optional)

(attach your art, important)

Though, if you want to stay anonymous, you can simply leave the username input in blank
You can change the subject of your e-mail to whatever you like, I don't mind

2) Do NOT send me NSFW drawings of my characters

The thing is, I prefer to keep this website a safe space (if that makes sense) and keeping away any NSFW stuff in it, suggestive drawings of my characters are fine though
Speaking of which, make sure to point out on your e-mail if your drawing is suggestive or not, but this is purely optional, I guess

3) Any kind of work is great, really!

Sketches, paintings, etc. are fine for me, hell even joke-ish drawings are acceptable. What can I say? Art is art!
